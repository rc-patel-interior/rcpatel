Types of Modular Kitchens We Design in Mumbai

L-Shaped Modular Kitchen

Perfect for medium-sized kitchens. Maximises corner space with efficient work triangle between sink, stove, and refrigerator.

U-Shaped Modular Kitchen

Ideal for larger kitchens. Provides maximum storage and counter space on three walls for serious home cooks.

Parallel / Galley Kitchen

Efficient and space-saving design with cabinets and appliances on two parallel walls. Great for long, narrow kitchens.

Straight / Single Wall Kitchen

Best for compact 1BHK and studio apartments. Clean, minimal design that fits in limited space.

Island Kitchen

A premium open kitchen design with a central island for cooking, dining, or extra storage. Perfect for large open-plan homes.

Peninsula Kitchen

A space-efficient variation of the island kitchen, connected to one wall, great for semi-open floor plans.
